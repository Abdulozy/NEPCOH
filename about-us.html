About Network for Peace, Cohesion & Heritage Centre in Mandera County

OUR MANDATE

The Regional Peace, Cohesion, and Heritage Centre is to: Provide daily conflict early warning and early response, data collection, verification, and analysis. Aid conflict rapid response to the population in the Mandera triangle. Offer training, guidance, and mentorship to the various peacebuilding Infrastructures. Provide daily contact points for county officials and community interlocutors. Coordinate all activities of grassroot structures like the Ceasefire Monitoring Committees (CMCs), Intervillage Dialogue Spaces etc. Provide secretarial support to the Peace Actor's Forums. Contribute to the efforts of the international community in crisis prevention and stability by offering secretarial support to IGAD's cross border Conflict Prevention, Resolution and Management (CPRM) cluster in the triangle (Kenya, Somalia, and Ethiopia)

Offer research, policy and learning opportunities that incubates local contextually appropriate peacebuilding and conflict management frameworks for pastoral communities. Incubate best practices in conflict management that are contextually appropriate in pastoral conflicts. Offer an important knowledge reference learning centre for peacebuilding, and on issues around fragility and resilience in Kenya and the region. Be a central platform where Interpeace will share its over 25 years of experience in peacebuilding with the Mandera triangle regional administrations and other actors in the region.

OUR TEAM

The membership of the Trust is premised on Networks of Peacebuilding experts, organizations and Individual of all genders regardless of their age, ethnicity, political, or religious affiliations.

Leadership

Four Representatives from grassroot Community peace structure, County Government, National government or NGOs all working in Peacebuilding sector.

Three Technical Experts i.e. Business community member, Financial expert and expert in Peacebuilding and Regional cooperation. The board plays key role in defining NEPCOH Trust overall policy and strategy. The board also serves as the legal authority that is responsible for the organization.
